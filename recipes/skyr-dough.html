Skyr Dough

Photo by Phil Hearing on Unsplash

Description

A very quick and easy protein-packed dough, perfect for pizza!

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 7 g baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 250g plain skyr

Steps

  1. Sift flour into a bowl. Add baking powder and salt.
  2. Thoroughly mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add skyr and mix everything by hand.
  4. Knead until the dough is one smooth ball.
